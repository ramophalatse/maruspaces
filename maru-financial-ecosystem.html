March 2026 Version 1.1 Prepared by: Ramo (Fractional CFO/COO)
Lean Essential components only. No redundant tools or duplicate data entry.
Intelligent Components link to one another. Automation over manual re-keying.
Scalable Grows with the business. Scope and complexity add without restructuring.
01 · Set
Strategic anchor
Budget & tracker
  • Income targets by service line & client segment
  • Full opex budget — fixed & variable
  • Annual company targets (monthly P&L cascade)
  • Revenue model: target vs actual comparison
  • Actuals performance tracker
  • Variable budget monthly log
  • Spend breakdown by category
  • Sets the destination for all layers below

The strategic starting point · defines what success looks like · all downstream layers measure performance against these targets

targets cascade down
02 · Capture
Automation
Sage AutoEntry
  • Scans supplier invoices & receipts
  • Auto-codes line items to GL categories
  • Pushes coded entries to Sage Accounting
  • Eliminates manual invoice capture

Input: supplier docs · Output: coded GL entries

Payroll
Sage Payroll
  • Payslip generation & distribution
  • EMP201 / PAYE submissions to SARS
  • UIF contributions & IRP5 certificates
  • Leave management & HR records
  • Staff cost journals → Sage Accounting

Salary lines feed Budget's Human Capital section

Manual
Bank & manual inputs
  • Client payments received (bank feed)
  • Adhoc income entries
  • Exceptional / once-off expenses
  • Opening balances & corrections

Reconciled monthly between Sage and Money Metrics

raw inputs flow to ledger
03 · Record
System of record
Sage Business Cloud Accounting
  • General ledger & chart of accounts
  • Invoicing, debtors & creditors
  • Bank reconciliation
  • Project / job costing codes
  • VAT201 source data (→ Jacobus)
  • EMP201 / PAYE reconciliation
  • Annual financial statements
  • Statutory compliance anchor

Primary source of truth · all capture-layer tools feed here · downstream tools draw actuals from here

actuals feed operational tracking
04 · Manage
Fallback record
Money Metrics
  • Independent transaction log (6,768+ rows)
  • Fixed & variable transaction setup
  • Adhoc income & exceptional expense capture
  • Budget vs actual tracker
  • Fallback if Sage is disrupted
  • Primary data source for dashboard

Second source of truth · independent of Sage · feeds the Money Metrics Dashboard

Project-level
Monthly cost sheets
  • Per-project gross profit tracker
  • Revenue vs COS/COGS per invoice
  • Up to 2 supplier cost lines per entry
  • Real-time GP% by project
  • Invoice-linked records
  • Separate tab per client / project

GP actuals feed up to Budget Actuals tab and down to management reports

actuals + commitments inform forward view
05 · Plan
Forward-looking
Cashflow forecast
  • Monthly income & expense projection
  • Opening / closing cash balance
  • Surplus / deficit signal by month
  • SARS payment plan scheduling
  • Payment timing gate (pre-payment check)
  • Settlement & director's loan planning

Decision gate: no large disbursements without cashflow clearance

Compliance
Compliance calendar
  • VAT201 submission deadlines (bi-monthly)
  • EMP201 / PAYE monthly deadlines
  • Provisional tax dates (Aug + Feb)
  • SARS payment arrangement instalments
  • Annual financial statement due dates
  • CIPC annual return filing
  • SACAP / SACPCM registration renewals

Tab within Cashflow Forecast · owner: Ramo · reviewed monthly with Jacobus

synthesised outputs for decision-making

Money Metrics dashboard
  • Visual P&L and cashflow summary
  • Income vs expense trends (by year)
  • 50/30/20 spend breakdown
  • Top expenses ranking
  • Interactive date, category & type filters

Source: Money Metrics sheet · shared with Maps at monthly review

Narrative
Management reports
  • Monthly target vs actual P&L
  • Project-level GP summary (from cost sheets)
  • Cashflow position & 3-month outlook
  • Compliance status (from compliance calendar)
  • Key decisions & open items

Monthly cadence · produced by Ramo · reviewed with Maps
